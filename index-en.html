About Us

Tacoma Unitas is an association of logistics companies. We will become a reliable partner for your business, as we have vast experience and technical capabilities for comfortable work.

We have been working on the logistics services market for over 23 years. Furthermore, we export and import various goods to the countries of the EU, the CIS, Asia, and Scandinavia. We guarantee timely and reliable transportation of any cargo.

Services

We organize and accompany the transportation of goods by road transport; railway cars, sea containers.

The company's logistics cover the EU, CIS, Asia and Scandinavia. Delivery of goods to almost all seaports of the world.

We carry out passenger transportation by comfortable buses in the following directions:
  • Chernivtsi - Düsseldorf;
  • Chernivtsi - Oslo;
  • Chernivtsi - Riga;

We import wines from Bulgaria to the Baltic countries and Israel, sanitary ware from Poland and China to Ukraine.
We export sunflower oil from Ukraine to the EU, Turkey, Bulgaria and Africa; rapeseed, soybean and sunflower from Argentina and Brazil to Poland, Bulgaria, Lithuania, Turkey, Africa.

The company's path and strategic goals

  • Past

    In 1999, individual proprietor T. I. Fefchak launched its professional activities. In 2007, it was reorganized into the "International Information Carrier" enterprise. Since 2009, the company already had branches in Poland and Latvia. Since 2012, "Takoma Unitas" is a registered brand associated with reliability and responsibility.

  • Present

    Today, the logistics fund of the company "Takoma Unitas" includes: 548 machines, 8 buses, 6 wagons, 2 ships. The company employs over 600 highly qualified employees capable of transporting and escorting cargo around the world.

  • Plans

    In the near future, we are planning to open branches in Monaco and the USA for better logistics coverage of the world.
